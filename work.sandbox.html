Tick Tock Birthday Clock gives the user a countdown to their birthday, a place to create and store their birthday wishlist and check out their friend's wishlists.

Tick Tock Birthday Clock is built with JavaSCript. It uses mysql, bcrypt, dotenv, express, handlebars, bootstrap, session and sequelize. It's deployed on Heroku.
